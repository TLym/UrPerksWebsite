Helping Real Estate Agents Build The Most Valuable Farm They've Ever Owned


A Farm of Business Owners


As real estate agents, we have all been told that one of the most valuable farms you can build for your real estate business is a network of business owners.

Business owners tend to be higher earners, and that is reflected in the price tag on the real estate they own. Additionally, they are more likely to own multiple pieces of real estate (their personal residence, business property, and other investment properties). They also tend to have a larger network of people who they influence (other local business owners in addition to their friends and family).

A farm of business owners is clearly valuable.

The question is: how do you build one?
